Opportunity Cost Analysis

Understanding Opportunity Cost

Opportunity cost represents the potential investment growth that could have been achieved if the taxes paid for Roth conversions were instead invested in a taxable account. The calculator applies capital gains tax only to the growth above the original tax payments, rather than assuming taxes are paid annually on unrealized gains. This analysis helps determine whether the tax-free growth of Roth assets outweighs the investment potential of the tax payments.

Key Insight: The Roth conversion is beneficial when the final Roth value minus opportunity cost exceeds the traditional IRA after-tax value.
